Smart pet feeder(info)

An automatic smart pet feeder is an astonishing gadget where a specific pet is being automatically treated their meals at predetermined times whether the owner is engaged with hustling schedules or not. Invented by. Raymond V. Sweeny in 1939, these Automatic feeders help provide proper weight management by giving your pet the portioned feedings they need besides being an assistant robot for feeding your pets. For instance; If you have a pet who gulps his food or eats too fast, a feeder can help him slow down. The Slow Feed option with the new Simply Feed pet feeder dispenses each meal over a 15-minute period.

To operate the AutoDiet SMART feeder simply attach a wireless tag to the collar of each pet. When a pet attempts to feed it will be instantly recognized and registered. Once registered, you can program the feeder so that it welcomes your pet by name and allow or exclude pets as you desire.
