Apple watch(info)

In 2014 Apple watch was presented by the CEO Tim Cook in conjunction with iPhone 6, it was marked as fashion, accessory. It officially released in April 2015 it is also made for more healthy. Apple decided to make a watch and only then set out to discover what it might be good for you “There was a sense that technology was going to move onto the body”, said by Alan Dye, who runs Apple’s human interface group.

There is a massive feature on apple watch called “The digital crown”, which is the rotating button on the side of Apple Watch. Press and hold the Digital Crown to use Siri, turn it to scroll or zoom, press it to view the Watch face or Home Screen, and double-press to open the last used app.

Well, that’s only starting, there are so many other cool beneficial attributes and quality present here. One of them is being water resistant. Your Apple Watch is water resistant, not waterproof. Therefore, as an example, you can wear and use your Apple Watch during exercise (exposure to sweat is OK), in the rain, and while washing your hands. Nevertheless, it is not completely waterproof.
